About the Jewler!

Hello! My name is Jackie Pyke and I have a small self run jewellery buissness. I love making jewellery and it has been my hobbie for 25 years.
I sell my jewllery at different markets and a do classes and teach people how to do some jewlery every once in a while.
My jewellery brings me joy and seeing how people react when they look at my jewllery brings me more joy.
I make jewllery for all ages, even young children. I always have a fun little charm and put them on a small chain or a colourful ribbons.
I also make things like keychains, christmas oderments, bookmarks, ect. I feel like with making jewellery, you don't just make bracelets and neclaces, you make things that will intrest everyone.
